Jakarta Islamic Boys Boarding School is a school in Bogor, West Java. JIBBS was founded in 2005 with 11 initial students. At the beginning of its development, JIBBS only had 1 classroom, 1 computer lab, 2 student dormitories, 1 shared dining room, 2 offices, 1 owner's house, 1 field. The 2nd year of JIBBS added 1 class. Continuing since entering the 4th year, JIBBS began to enlarge the land area followed by moving the dormitory space.
